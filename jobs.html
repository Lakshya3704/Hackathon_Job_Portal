List Of Jobs With Description

    IT and Software

    1. Software engineering

    A Software Engineer is an IT professional who designs, develops and maintains computer software at a company. They use their creativity and technical skills and apply the principles of software engineering to help solve new and ongoing problems for an organization.

    Requirements and skills
  1. Proven work experience as a Software Engineer or Software Developer.
  2. Experience designing interactive applications
  3. Ability to develop software in Java, Ruby on Rails, C++ or other programming languages
  4. Excellent knowledge of relational databases, SQL and ORM technologies (JPA2, Hibernate)
  5. Experience developing web applications using at least one popular web framework (JSF, Wicket, GWT, Spring MVC)

    2. Devops Engineer

    A DevOps engineer is responsible for the smooth operation of a company's IT infrastructure. They work with developers to deploy and manage code changes, and with operations staff to ensure that systems are up and running smoothly. To be successful in this role, a DevOps engineer must have a deep understanding of both development and operations processes, as well as a strong technical background.

    Requirements and skills
  1. Experience working on Linux based infrastructure
  2. Excellent understanding of Ruby, Python, Perl, and Java
  3. Configuration and managing databases such as MySQL, Mongo
  4. Working knowledge of various tools, open-source technologies, and cloud services
  5. Awareness of critical concepts in DevOps and Agile principles

    3. Security Engineer

    A Security Engineer, or Security Administrator, handles the digital security for their company or clients. Their duties include building digital security protocols, operating a cyber security system and maintaining an IT security infrastructure for their organization or their clients’ organizations.

    Requirements and skills
  1. Design computer security strategy and engineer comprehensive cybersecurity architecture.
  2. Identify, define and document system security requirements and recommend solutions to management.
  3. Configure, troubleshoot and maintain security infrastructure software and hardware.
  4. Working knowledge of various tools, open-source technologies, and cloud services
  5. Awareness of critical concepts in DevOps and Agile principles

    4. Data Scientist

    A Data Scientist is a professional who collects large amounts of data using analytical, statistical, and programmable skills. It is their responsibility to use data to develop solutions tailored to meet the organisation's unique needs.

    Requirements and skills
  1. knowledge of statistical programming languages like R, Python, and database query languages like SQL, Hive, Pig is desirable.
  2. Good applied statistical skills, including knowledge of statistical tests, distributions, regression, maximum likelihood estimators, etc
  3. Machine Learning good knowledge of machine learning methods like k-Nearest Neighbors, Naive Bayes, SVM, Decision Forests.
  4. Multivariable Calculus and Linear Algebra
  5. Hands-on experience with data science tools

    5. Java Developer

    A Java Developer is a programmer who designs, develops, and manages Java-based applications and software. With most large organizations using Java to implement software systems and backend services, a Java developer is one of the most sought-after jobs today.

    Requirements and skills
  1. Proficiency in Java, with a good understanding of its ecosystems
  2. Sound knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) Patterns and Concepts
  3. amiliarity with different design and architectural patterns
  4. Basic Understanding of the concepts of MVC (Model-View-Controller) Pattern, JDBC (Java Database Connectivity), and RESTful web services
  5. Relevant Knowledge of Java GUI frameworks like Swing, SWT, AWT according to project requirements

    Business Administration and Marketing

    1. Accountant

    An Accountant helps businesses make critical financial decisions by collecting, tracking, and correcting the company’s finances. They are responsible for financial audits, reconciling bank statements, and ensuring financial records are accurate throughout the year.

    Requirements and skills
  1. Excellent knowledge of accounting regulations and procedures, including the Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP)
  2. Hands-on experience with accounting software like FreshBooks and QuickBooks
  3. Advanced MS Excel skills including Vlookups and pivot tables
  4. Experience with general ledger functions
  5. BSc in Accounting, Finance or relevant degree

    2. Business Consultant

    A Business Consultant is an expert who offers advice, information, insights, and recommendations to assist clients in achieving their objectives and resolving challenges. They provide guidance on various aspects of business to optimize performance and drive success.

    Requirements and skills
  1. Knowledge of diverse business matters such as IT, Marketing, HR etc.
  2. Familiarity with a wide selection of business management software (CRM, ERP etc.) and databases
  3. Analytical mind with excellent data collection and analysis skills
  4. BSc/BA in business administration or relevant field; MSc/MA in a specialized
  5. Certified Management Consultant is a plus

    3. Business manager

    A Business Manager is a professional who is responsible for leading and supervising employees to ensure productivity efficiency of operations and providing direction on how best to handle different tasks while maintaining customer satisfaction. Business Managers help implement strategies that will help generate revenue or profitability.

    Requirements and skills
  1. Excellent organizational and leadership skills
  2. Excellent knowledge of MS Office, databases and information systems
  3. Good understanding of research methods and data analysis techniques
  4. Excellent knowledge of MS Office, databases and information systems
  5. BSc/Ba in Business Management or relevant field; MSc/MA will be a plus

    4. Chief executie Officer

    The Chief Executive Officer (CEO) is the highest-ranking executive of any company. They are responsible for ensuring that the business operates at a profit and meets its goals. They need to know how best to approach new opportunities, including delegating tasks or directing agendas to drive profitability by managing organizational structure.

    Requirements and skills
  1. Experience in developing profitable strategies and implementing vision
  2. Strong understanding of corporate finance and performance management principles
  3. Familiarity with diverse business functions such as marketing, PR, finance etc.
  4. In-depth knowledge of corporate governance and general management best practices
  5. An entrepreneurial mindset with outstanding organizational and leadership skills

    5. Chief Financial Officer

    A Chief Financial Officer (CFO) is a senior executive responsible for managing the financial actions of their company. They are often in charge of tracking cash flow, analyzing strengths/weaknesses in the company’s finances and overseeing all aspects of its financial success.

    Requirements and skills
  1. In depth knowledge of corporate financial law and risk management practices
  2. Proficient in the use of MS Office and financial management software (e.g. SAP)
  3. Strong leadership and organizational skills
  4. An analytical mind, comfortable with numbers
  5. CPA is a strong advantage
